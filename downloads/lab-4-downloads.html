Assignment Instructions

🎯 Learning Objectives

By completing this lab, students will:

  1. Analyze productivity data from hands-on economic experiments
  2. Calculate productivity changes using economic formulas
  3. Define key concepts including division of labor, specialization, and comparative advantage
  4. Apply theoretical knowledge to real-world economic scenarios
  5. Reflect critically on the relationship between cooperation and economic efficiency

📋 Submission Requirements

  • Due: 48 hours after in-class experiment
  • Points: 20 total
  • Format: Choose from interactive completion, PDF submission, or markdown file
  • Length: Complete responses to all sections (approximately 3-4 pages when printed)

📊 Grading Criteria

Category Points Requirements
Data Collection 4 pts Complete and accurate experimental data
Calculations 4 pts Correct productivity formulas and math
Concept Definitions 4 pts Clear, accurate economic definitions
Real-world Examples 4 pts Relevant, well-explained applications
Analysis & Reflection 4 pts Thoughtful, well-reasoned responses
